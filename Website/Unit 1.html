This unit helps me to understand the main technologies and processes benind the internet and investigate how they come together to let you view websites and send information across the world. The internet and web of tomorrow wil be even more powerful, more connected, more intuitive and a more important part of our lives. This results in an internet of services, objects and infrastructure (ubiquitous computing) which will radically change our lives. For example, smart appliances will be able to talk to each other, clothes will monitor our health and retailers will access social media to gain insight into shoppers' preferences. This unit is an online World that helps us to understand what is the in online world and to investigate the use of online that allows you to participate in its activities and communicate with others as well as sending information and sharing with others information. And how it processes behind the internet.

Learning aims in this unit you will:
  • investigate online services and online communication
  • investigate components of the internet and how digital devices exchange and store information
  • investigate issues with operating online
